Welcome to my working notes

This is a collection of my research notes that I share publicly. I work with "the garage door open" and share my thoughts on my ongoing work early on in the process.

Working notes vs. blog

The notes on this website are fundamentally different than, say, a blog post or a full-fledged article. Importantly, they are subject to major revisions and may be different every time you re-visit them. Some are in an early stage and only capture a rough thought; others have undergone several rounds of revision and are more structured (From seedling to evergreen).

They are also different in terms of the target audience. In contrast to a blog post, my writing here is mostly aimed at myself, but I allow you to look over my shoulders and make comments (you can reach me via my website). That is, although some notes are well(ish)-structured, they may still not be self-contained. I do my best to provide sufficient information, but at the end of the day, I write primarily for myself.

How to use

This website is somewhat unusual and different to use (also, sorry for the poor layout; learning CSS has no priority for me at the moment). As you will realize, I put a strong emphasis on bi-directional links, connecting notes back and forth (Bi-directional links emulate the thinking process). This is a key element and adds to the experience of roaming through my thought processes. Every linked note contains a backlink (at the "References" section at the very bottom), which returns to the original note. But it also contains backlinks to every other note that is connected to the linked note. This creates a web of connected thoughts and invites you to get lost in my thinking.

The website is also unusual because it probably contains a lot or typos. Sorry, not my strength.
